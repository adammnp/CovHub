What is Covid-19?

Coronaviruses (CoV) are a widespread pathogen family that causes symptoms ranging from the common cold to pneumonia (a more severe lung infection). COVID-19 is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 coronavirus strain. The World Health Organization labeled it a pandemic in March 2020, and it has spread internationally.
